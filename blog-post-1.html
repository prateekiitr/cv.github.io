Conclusion: The CNN Mindset

Whether processing 2D chest X-rays or 1D ECG traces, the CNN approach remains consistent:

  1. Local feature extraction through sliding kernels
  2. Hierarchical abstraction via deep layers
  3. Domain-specific adaptation of kernel shapes and network topology

This flexibility explains why CNNs remain indispensable in my work at Samsung, whether developing smartphone health features or automotive safety systems.
